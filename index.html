The Classroom 🎒

In a small village northern Iran , young Ahmad listens as the teacher criticizes his classmate Mohammad for not doing his homework in the correct notebook. 📖

The teacher warns that another mistake will cause him to be expelled. 😔

The Discovery 🏃‍♂️

After school, Ahmad finds Mohammad’s notebook in his schoolbag. 🎒 Realizing his friend will be punished, he makes a decision.
